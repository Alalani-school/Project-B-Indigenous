Colours

What colours are on here?

The colours on this image include red, white, and black-blue. Most of these colours are used by European compaines making racist logos towards the Indigenous.

What do they represent?

These colours could show the red indian stereotype (see stereotypes page) and inacurately represent the Indigenous as it shows them as white with black/blue hair.
The red also affiliates the Indigenous people with blood, and is an insult to Indigenous culture.
