Assumptions Made

The Colour Red

One assumption in this image is the use of the colour red. This colour is being used as the highlight of
the image, which is a reference to the Red Indian Stereotype, and assumes that the Indigenous are savage and covered in blood.

Half-Clothed Indigenous

Another assumption being made is that the Indigenous are half clothed, as shown in the logo. This assumption is incorrect, as the Indigenous are nearly always properly clothed.

Blue-Black haired Indigenous

Yet another assumption being made here is that the Indigenous had blue-black hair, seen in the logo. This version of reality is almost always not true, as barely anyone has that hair colour.

White Indigenous People

The last assumption being made here is that the Indigenous people are white. This is certainly not true, as there would have been quite a
bit less racism towards them, and in nearly all photos of the Indigenous it shows them as brown.
